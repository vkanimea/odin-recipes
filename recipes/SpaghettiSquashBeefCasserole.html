Spaghetti Squash Beef Casserole

Description

An evening dish that can be served with glass of wine

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons butter, melted
  • 1 spaghetti squash, halved and seeded
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes, drained
  • ½ (8 ounce) package sliced fresh mushrooms
  • ¼ cup diced red onion
  • ¼ cup diced green bell pepper
  • 1 clove garlic, chopped
  • ½ teaspoon dried oregano
  • ½ teaspoon dried basil
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 pinch dried thyme
  • 2 ½ cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup crushed buttery round crackers

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C)
  2. Brush butter lightly over squash halves. Cover with aluminum foil
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until easily pierced with a fork, about 40 minutes. Cool until easily handled, about 15 minutes
  4. Preheat a large skillet over medium heat. Add ground beef; cook and stir until browned, about 5 minutes
  5. Stir in tomatoes, mushrooms, red onion, green bell pepper, garlic, oregano, basil, salt, pepper, and thyme; cook, stirring occasionally, until flavors combine
  6. 6 to 8 minutes Scrape squash flesh into the skillet; mix into the beef mixture
  7. Spoon into a large casserole dish. Fold in mozzarella cheese; top with crushed crackers
  8. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, about 40 minutes
